Robo Match is a memory card game where you match pairs of robots. There are 12 cards, each with a robot image on one side.

  1. All cards are face down at start of the game.
  2. Click on a card to flip it over and reveal the robot.
  3. Click on another card to flip it over and reveal the second robot.
  4. If the two robots match, they will remain face up.
  5. If the robots do not match, they will flip back over after a short delay.
  6. Continue flipping cards until all pairs are matched.
  7. Try to match all pairs in the fewest number of moves.
  8. Try again.

Good luck and have fun!
